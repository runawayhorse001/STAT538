problem 16.12 e

  • Hypothesis:

  • \(H_0\): all the \(\mu_i, i= 1,\cdots,5\) are equal.

  • \(H_\alpha\): not all \(\mu_i, i= 1,\cdots,5\) are equal.

  • Decision rules: From the above ANOVA table , we get \(F^*\) = 1107.5/7.5 = 147.6667; Now calculate F(0.90; 4, 95):

qf(0.90, 4, 95)
## [1] 2.004992

If \(F^*\) is less or equal F(0.90; 4, 95)= 2.0049918, conclude \(H_0\), otherwise \(H_\alpha\). Based on this criteria, since \(F^*\) = 147.6667 > 2.0049918, conclude \(H_\alpha\).
