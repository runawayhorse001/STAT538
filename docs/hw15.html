## Interpretation

  • Interpret the c statistic: The C-statistic is a measure of goodness of fit for binary outcomes in a logistic regression model. A value below 0.5 indicates a very poor model. A value of 0.5 means that the model is no better than predicting an outcome than random chance. Values over 0.7 indicate a good model. Values over 0.8 indicate a strong model. A value of 1 means that the model perfectly predicts those group members who will experience a certain outcome and those who will not. From the Roc Curve, we may conclude that the model is no better than predicting on outcome than random chance.
  • Interpret the odds ratio for PEP_STAR: the coefficient of oadds ratio for PEP_STAR is 1.3604636 which means, when PEP_STAR increases 1 unit and the odds will increase 1.3604636 units when the other parameters are fixed.
  • Interpret the effect plot for RECENT_AVG_GIFT_AMT: the coefficient of oadds ratio for PEP_STAR is 0.9944644 which means, when RECENT_AVG_GIFT_AMT increases 1 unit and the odds will increase 0.9944644 units when the other parameters are fixed.
